Olympic Games

Analysis

The women have had a greater impact and participation in all types of sports in recent decades, and the Winter Olympic Games can demonstrate the increase in this participation over the years. It is true that male participation has won a large part of the Olympic medals in different disciplines, but it can be seen how it has tried to reach a gender balance with the increase in female participation after 1994.
